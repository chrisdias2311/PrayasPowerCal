Proximate Analysis


Proximate analysis is the technique used to analyze the moisture content, content of volatile matter, ash content and content of fixed carbon of coal.





Enter the weight of coal taken, Weight of coal after moisture loss, weight of coal after volatile matter loss, weight of residue, and the app will calculate the %Moisture,%Volatile matter, % Ash and %Fixed Carbon
